Finding Red Betty

The rear of 859 Sydney Road, Brunswick.

Red Betty is tucked down Houdini Lane behind 859 Sydney Road. We recommend entering via Cozens Street. You'll see our posters and the welcome red glow of our neon.

The Stage

Red Betty's stage is magic

Our stage was built by magician Dane Certificate to perform his magic. It's an ideal space for a band or small performances. Contact us if you are looking for a boutique space.

Private functions/screenings

Ideal for film nights and screenings

With our in-built digital projector, screen and stage it's easy to turn the space into your own screening room.

Important note

No EFTPOS

We are still organising EFTPOS facilities, so for now we are cash only. There is a Commonwealth Bank ATM on the corner of Sydney Road and Moreland Road.
